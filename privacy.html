Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Video Compressor: Shrink Videos ("the App") as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All video compression is performed entirely on your device. Your videos, photos, and files never leave your iPhone.

Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to select videos for compression and to save compressed videos back to your library. We do not upload, analyze, or share any of your media.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (weekly subscription and lifetime unlock) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all billing and transaction processing.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All app preferences and compression history are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, this applies universally.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
